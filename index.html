About

New York City is one of the largest and most dense cities in the United States. It is comprised of five boroughs, which are the Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and Staten Island. With a population of over 8 million people, it is the most populous city in the United States. New York City is also a major tourist destination, with over 60 million visitors per year. The city is known as melting pot of various cultures.
